Who Am I?

Name: Kenneth Laurito

Status: Single, 3rd Year BMMA Student

Mission: My mission is to master the arts of multimedia and design while perfecting the craft of Gunpla building. As a Multimedia Arts student, I view every model kit not just as a toy, but as a lesson in mechanical aesthetics and color theory. The patience required to sand down nubs and apply water-slide decals translates directly into my design workflow teaching me that the smallest details often make the biggest impact. Ultimately, I aim to merge these worlds, using my photography and video editing skills to showcase my builds in cinematic ways.
