The Art of Gunpla

Gunpla (Gundam Plastic Model) is more than just assembling toys, it is an art form. It involves cutting parts from runners, sanding down nubs, panel lining for depth, and applying decals to bring the machine to life.

My Favorite Mobile Suit

The crown jewel of my interest is the Full Armor Gundam (Thunderbolt Ver.).

From a design perspective, it represents the absolute peak of 'realistic' mecha mechanics. Unlike traditional hero robots that look sleek and clean, this machine is bulky, industrial, and utilitarian. It features four independent shields for omni-directional defense, vinyl joint covers to protect against space debris, and a massive backpack equipped with sub-arms. The complex layering of weapons and thrusters creates a dense, intimidating silhouette that is a nightmare to animate but a dream to build.
